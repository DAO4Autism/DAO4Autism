Supporting Neurodiverse Parenting Through Web3

🌱 Our Mission

DAO4Autism is a decentralized, Web3-based storytelling initiative supporting parents of autistic and neurodiverse children. We focus on emotional storytelling, anonymous sharing, and digital creativity—empowering caregivers to connect, reflect, and heal together.
