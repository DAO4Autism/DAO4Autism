DAO4Autism

Supporting Neurodiverse Parenting Through Web3

🌱 Our Mission

DAO4Autism is a Web3 storytelling initiative that supports parents of autistic and neurodiverse children through emotional sharing, decentralized privacy, and creative empowerment. Our goal is to help future generations of parents feel seen, even when they feel invisible.
