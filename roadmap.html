DAO4Autism Roadmap

Our roadmap outlines the planned milestones and phases for DAO4Autism, building a lasting, decentralized support system for parents of neurodiverse children.

Phase 1: Foundation (Q3 2025)

  • Finalize project concept and team roles
  • Build and launch the DAO4Autism website
  • Publish initial emotionally-driven e-book chapters on Mirror.xyz
  • Establish social media presence (Twitter, GitHub, Mirror)

Phase 2: Content & Community Growth (Q4 2025)

  • Publish remaining e-book chapters (total 17 chapters)
  • Produce and release short AI-voiced videos with copyright-free visuals
  • Launch the “Stories Gallery” for shared parental narratives
  • Begin community engagement on Discord, Farcaster, Reddit

Phase 3: Platform Development & DAO Launch (Q1–Q2 2026)

  • Design and develop decentralized platform for anonymous parental support
  • Test privacy and security features with early users
  • Establish DAO governance mechanisms for community participation
  • Host AMA sessions, mentorship programs, and online events

Phase 4: Expansion & Sustainability (Q3 2026 and Beyond)

  • Scale community with onboarding tools and partnerships
  • Develop long-term funding via Gitcoin Grants, crowdfunding, and donations
  • Release advanced content formats and interactive features
  • Foster a self-sustaining ecosystem of parent-to-parent support

Join us in building a supportive, lasting home for neurodiverse parents.
