DAO4Autism

A Web3 space for emotional healing, parental stories, and digital solidarity

🧩 Welcome to DAO4Autism

DAO4Autism is a community-driven, decentralized initiative supporting the emotional journey of parents raising autistic and neurodiverse children. We aim to offer a safe, anonymous, and expressive space—through storytelling, digital creativity, and long-term mental health advocacy.

🌱 Why This Project Matters

Most autism-related platforms focus on children’s needs—which is vital—but often forget the emotional burdens of caregiving. Parents experience chronic stress, grief, isolation, and silent struggles that don't always have a name. DAO4Autism exists to change that narrative.

We believe every caregiver deserves a place to feel heard, not fixed. A place to process, not perform. And a place where emotional honesty matters more than perfection.

🚧 What We’re Building

  • 📘 A multi-chapter emotional e-book sharing real, reflective stories of autism parenting
  • 🎥 Short, creative videos using AI voiceovers to visualize inner struggles and unseen caregiving
  • 🛠️ A future decentralized platform for anonymous emotional support, sharing, and community healing
  • 🤝 Participation in Gitcoin Grants and Mirror crowdfund to grow this grassroots initiative
