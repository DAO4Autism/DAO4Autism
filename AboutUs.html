🧩 About Us

DAO4Autism is a parent-first, emotionally grounded project born out of lived experience, deep listening, and silent caregiving.

Behind this initiative is a small, multidisciplinary team led by a healthcare professional with years of voluntary experience supporting autism families. While we remain pseudonymous to preserve the trust of families we’ve worked with — and to protect the privacy of the stories shared with us — our purpose is clear:

🌱 We exist to build a safe, decentralized platform where autism parents can express, learn, and connect — without fear of judgment or exposure.

We’ve seen firsthand how existing forums often focus solely on the child — and while that is vital, they often overlook the emotional labor, burnout, and invisible grief carried by parents. In autism, there is no single solution — and that’s precisely why parents need more than advice: they need belonging.

DAO4Autism is not about diagnosing or fixing. It’s about holding space. It’s about creating a Web3-native refuge where stories matter more than labels, and connection heals more than prescriptions.

What We're Working On:

  • 📘 A series of emotionally rich e-book chapters based on real parenting struggles.
  • 🎥 Short videos with AI voiceovers and copyright-free visuals to bring unspoken feelings to light.
  • 🛡️ A future decentralized platform where privacy, solidarity, and shared learning become the pillars of a stronger autism parenting community.

We may be pseudonymous — but our commitment is very real.
We build slowly, carefully, and with the hope that what we’re building today will support the hearts of tomorrow’s caregivers.
